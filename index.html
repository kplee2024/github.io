About

Professor Kevin P. Lee is a leading scholar in law and technology, with a special focus on artificial intelligence, ethics, and jurisprudence. With over two decades of experience in legal academia and a background in philosophy, religion, and public ethics, his work bridges traditional legal theory with emerging challenges in digital society.
